THE ARTIST

Manoj Kumar of Doha, a versatile artist, expresses his lifelong passion through watercolor, acrylic, oil, pencil, sculpture, and photography. His emotive art captivates in competitions, exhibitions, and collaborations, reflecting his profound journey

ABOUT THE ART

Accomplished artist Manoj Kumar of Doha transcends language with evocative works, expressing profound thoughts and emotions through watercolor, acrylic, oil, pencil, sculpture, and photography. Inspired by Qatar's art scene.
